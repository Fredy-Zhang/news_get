    <h1 class="post-title">US security guard murdered following argument over face mask</h1>
    <p class="article-heading-des">A woman, her adult son and husband have been charged in the fatal shooting in Michigan.</p>
    <div class="article-heading-author-wrap">
      <div class="article-heading-author-img">
        <div class="article-heading-author-name">
          <div class="article-duration"><time class="timeagofunction" datetime="Mon May 04 2020 22:36:56 GMT+0000">04 May 2020 22:36 GMT</time></div>
        </div>
      </div>

    <div class="article-readToMe-share-block" id="article-top-share-icon">

</div></div></div></div></div></div><div></div></div></div></div><div><div><div><div style="width:100%"><div><div class="row article-main-content-wrapper"><div><div><div><div style="width:100%"><div><div class="col-md-8 article-main-content"><div><div style="border-style:none"><article id="main-article-block" class="main-article-block clear-fix"><div class="main-article-body"><figure class="main-article-mediaCaption"><div class="main-article-media"><img class="img-responsive main-article-media-img" src="/mritems/imagecache/mbdxxlarge/mritems/Images/2020/5/4/3b40460c974045ff8f990098e5279673_18.jpg" alt="Undated booking photo provided by the Genesee County prosecutor's office showing Sharmel Teague [Genesee County Prosecutor's Office via AP Photo]" title="US security guard murdered following argument over face mask"></div><figcaption class="main-article-caption">Undated booking photo provided by the Genesee County prosecutor's office showing Sharmel Teague [Genesee County Prosecutor's Office via AP Photo]</figcaption></figure><div id="inpage-more-on-block" class="inpage-auto-embed hidden-xs"></div>
    <div class="article-p-wrapper" id="body-200771816342556199"><p class="speakable">A woman, her adult son and husband have been charged in the fatal shooting of a security guard who refused to let her daughter enter a Family Dollar in Michigan because she was not wearing a face mask to protect against transmission of the <a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/topics/events/coronavirus-outbreak.html">coronavirus</a>.</p>
<p class="speakable">Calvin Munerlyn was shot Friday at the store just north of downtown Flint a short time after telling Sharmel Teague's daughter she had to leave because she lacked a mask, according to Genesee County Prosecutor David Leyton.</p>
<h4>More:</h4>
<ul>
<li>
<h4><a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2020/04/coronavirus-lockdown-drains-parents-exposes-digital-divide-200422202633366.html?utm_source=website&utm_medium=article_page&utm_campaign=read_more_links">US coronavirus lockdowns drain parents, expose 'digital divide'</a></h4>
</li>
<li>
<h4><a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2020/04/york-starts-antibody-testing-medical-front-line-workers-200426052131499.html?utm_source=website&utm_medium=article_page&utm_campaign=read_more_links">New York starts antibody testing for front-line medical workers</a></h4>
</li>
<li>
<h4><a href="https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2020/04/states-start-reopen-coronavirus-death-toll-tops-50000-200424132303845.html?utm_source=website&utm_medium=article_page&utm_campaign=read_more_links">US states start to reopen as coronavirus death toll tops 50,000</a>Â </h4>
</li>
</ul>
<p class="speakable">Teague, 45, argued with Munerlyn, 43, before leaving. Two men later came to the store.</p>
<p>Teague; her husband, Larry Teague, 44; and Ramonyea Bishop, 23; are charged with first-degree premeditated murder and gun charges.</p>
<p>Larry Teague also is charged with violating Governor Gretchen Whitmer's executive order mandating that all customers and employees must wear face coverings inside grocery stores, Leyton said.</p>
<p>Witnesses identified Bishop as the man who shot Munerlyn in the back of the head, Leyton said.</p>
<p>Sharmel Teague has been arrested. Police were looking for her husband and son.</p>
<p>No information has been released about the daughter, who has not been charged in the shooting.</p>
<p>"It is important that the governor's order be respected and adhered to, and for someone to lose their life over it is beyond comprehension," Leyton said earlier Monday in a statement.</p>
<p>On Thursday, gun-carrying protesters and other demonstrators rallied inside the state Capitol, calling for coronavirus-related restrictions to be lifted. Some protesters with guns - which are allowed in the statehouse - went to the Senate gallery. Some senators wore bulletproof vests.</p>
<p>As of Monday, Michigan has reported 43,754 confirmed COVID-19 virus cases and 4,049 deaths due to complications from the disease.</p>
<p>"The hostile tone that we have seen in recent days on television and in social media can permeate our society in ways we sometimes don't fully realize or anticipate," Leyton told reporters Monday. "Decisions like staying home when we can, wearing a mask when going to the store and staying a safe distance from those around us - these should not be political arguments. They don't necessitate acts of defiance, and we simply cannot devolve into an us-versus-them mentality."</p>

<p>About 150 people attended a candlelight vigil Sunday night. On Monday, a makeshift memorial was started outside the Family Dollar.</p>
<p>Munerlyn's mother, Bernadett, said she wants justice for her son.</p>
<p>"They didn't have to take my baby, and it wasn't that serious," she said. "All you people just have to do is listen to the law, listen to the governor. Just stay home. If you don't have to come out, then you wouldn't need a mask unless you're out getting groceries or necessities. All my baby was doing was his job working and doing his job."</p>
<p>Whitmer offered her condolences.</p>
<p>"It is incredibly sad that in this crisis that this life was lost," Whitmer told reporters Monday. "We are mindful of how important it is that people keep a level head, that we do the right thing protecting ourselves and others."</p></div>
