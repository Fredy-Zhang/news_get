                                        <p>The novel coronavirus first appeared in France on December 27, 2019, said Yves Cohen, an intensive care chief of a hospital in Paris, in an interview with local media on Sunday.</p>
<p>He said his medical team revisited test results of 24 patients with respiratory infections who tested negative for flu and other kinds of coronavirus in December and January, among them a male patient who showed positive for COVID-19.</p>
<p>The patients, he added, were tested in hospital on December 27<strong>,<span>&nbsp;</span></strong>a month before the first French cases were confirmed.&nbsp;</p>
<p>As of Monday, France has reported more than 168,000 cases of COVID-19 with 24,898 deaths nationwide.&nbsp;</p>                                                                                    <!--æ­£æåé¡µç»æ-->
