<p>Under new guidelines, children under 14 will be allowed outside for one hour per day between 9am and 9pm.</p>
<p>They must be supervised, stay within one kilometre of their home, and continue to observe social distancing rules.</p>
<p>Scientists around the world continue work to understand Covid-19, but observations up to now suggest that it has a minimal impact on the young.</p>
<p>Each adult will be permitted to accompany up to three children.</p><div class="advert-wrapper advert-wrapper--outstream"><div id="outstream" class="dfp-ad advert--outstream"></div></div>
<p>Schools across the country remain closed for the time being.</p>
<p>The announcement comes after Spain&#x27;s daily death toll from the virus fell to 288, its lowest level since March 21, following a peak on April 2 of 961.</p>
<p>New daily cases have also fallen to 3,995, down from around 8,000 at the start of this month.</p>
<p>Announcing the measures, prime minister Spanish Prime Minister Pedro Sanchez said: &quot;If we act with prudence, this first relief measure will be followed by another one a week later.&quot;</p><div class="advert-wrapper advert-wrapper--articlempu"><div id="articlempu" class="dfp-ad advert--inarticle"></div></div>
<p>He said that if the situation continues to improve until May 2, adults will be allowed out for daily walks or exercise.</p>
<p>“I would like to convey to you the importance of being cautious,&quot; he added.</p>
<p>&quot;This is not a race to see who is the first to reopen a shopping mall or a small business establishment.”</p>
<h2>&#x27;IT&#x27;S BEEN EMOTIONAL&#x27;</h2>
<p>A ministry document also recommended that Spain&#x27;s regions double their intensive care capacity to cope with possible increases in COVID-19 cases as lockdown measures are eased.</p>
<p>Families emerging from their homes responded positively to the new guidelines.</p>
<p>Lucia Ibanez, 9, out for a walk with her mother in Madrid, said she had missed the streets and the park and &quot;feeling the air on your face&quot; during lockdown.</p>
<p>&quot;I never thought I would miss school but I really miss it,&quot; she said.</p>
<p>Carla Marquez, out with her daughter near Las Canteras beach in Gran Canaria, said: &quot;It was totally necessary after 42 days.</p>
