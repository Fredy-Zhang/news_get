<p>The Central European nation of Poland could soon pass a law that will outlaw sexual education, and the consequent push for LGBTQ groups to &#8216;educate&#8217; children about sexual orientation, for children under 15 years of age.  </p><div id="ld-6670-5140"></div><script data-cfasync="false" src="/cdn-cgi/scripts/5c5dd728/cloudflare-static/email-decode.min.js"></script><script>(function(w,d,s,i){w.ldAdInit=w.ldAdInit||[];w.ldAdInit.push({slot:12803450786495846,size:[0, 0],id:"ld-6670-5140"});if(!d.getElementById(i)){var j=d.createElement(s),p=d.getElementsByTagName(s)[0];j.async=true;j.src="//cdn2.lockerdomecdn.com/_js/ajs.js";j.id=i;p.parentNode.insertBefore(j,p);}})(window,document,"script","ld-ajs");</script>



<p>According to Polish weekly newsmagazine<em>Wprost</em>, the new bill  known as the &#8220;Stop Pedophilia&#8221; law  aims to send sex educatorsto prison for up to five years.</p><div></div>



<p>Anna Blus, a researcher withAmnesty International,described the legislation as &#8220;outrageous&#8221; and &#8220;extremely vague and broad&#8230;This bill will put young people at risk,&#8221; Blus told DW on Wednesday. &#8220;It could have disastrous effects.&#8221;</p>



<p>Newly elected PiS lawmaker Marcin Ociepa claimedfears that educators may end up behind bars are just an &#8220;overinterpretation of the bill,&#8221; and saidhe said hesaw nothing bad aboutthe legislation, wrote DW.</p>

<p>&#8220;This only says that it is not allowed to encourage a person younger than 15 &#8230; to have sex or to conduct other sexual activities,&#8221; Ociepa told private radio TOK FM, reported DW.</p>



<p>The purpose of the legislation is to criminalize the promotion of underage sexual activity suggesting that sexual education teachers groom and familiarize children with homosexuality, according to<a href="https://www.lgbtqnation.com/2020/04/poland-may-start-sending-teachers-jail-turning-kids-gay/">LGBTQ Nation</a>, writes National File.</p>



<p>Supporters of the legislation wrote in a statement that The organizations and activists most involved in the promotion of sexual education in our country are the LGBT lobby.</p>

<p>In Western Europe, members of these movements involved in implementing sex education in schools were convicted of pedophilia, the document added.</p>



<p>LGBT activist Ola Kaczorek told<a href="https://www.reuters.com/article/us-poland-lgbt-education-trfn/poland-mulls-law-denouncing-sex-educators-as-paedophiles-and-gay-activists-idUSKCN21X2ZA">Reuters</a>: This would make impossible for us as educators to come into schools and teach kids about humans, about what makes us us, and whats gender identity or sexual orientation,&#8221; reported National File.</p>



<p>Usually school is not a friendly environment for non-heterosexual kids, but now it will be even harder, Kaczorek added.</p>
