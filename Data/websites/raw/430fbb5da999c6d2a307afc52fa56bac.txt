</P></div></div>

</P></div></div>
<div class="comp-box text-group"><div class="inner"><P> The posts include hashtags that in English translate to #ThankstoYouCampaign, #ThankstoYouChallenge and #ThankstoHealthWorkers and the names of three people to continue the challenge.
