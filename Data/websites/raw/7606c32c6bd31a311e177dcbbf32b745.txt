                <span><p><span style="color:#c0392b;"><strong>Update: 11:52 a.m., April 27 </strong></span></p>

<p>TAIPEI (Taiwan News) &mdash; Japanese media, citing the medical team treating North Korean leader Kim Jong Un, say the failure of initial surgery has left the 36-year-old in a vegetative state.</p>

<p><span style="background-color:null;">Amid conflicting reports about Kim&#39;s health,</span> Japanese weekly magazine Shukan Gendai <a href="https://gendai.ismedia.jp/articles/-/72122" target="_blank">reported</a> on Friday (April 24) that a Chinese medical team member on the mission to North Korea briefly explained the situation to its senior writer, Kondm Daisuke.</p>

<p>The medical expert said during a visit to the countryside earlier this month, Kim clutched his chest and fell to the ground. A doctor accompanying Kim immediately carried out CPR and took him to a nearby hospital for emergency care.</p>

<p>The doctor also requested a medical team from Beijing for assistance. At the hospital, prior to the arrival of the Chinese medical team, the North Korean doctor performed cardiac surgery but there were complications due to the hereditary dictator&#39;s obesity and the doctor&#39;s anxiety.</p>

<p>Meanwhile, <a href="http://www.reuters.com/article/us-northkorea-politics-exclusive/exclusive-china-sent-team-including-medical-experts-to-advise-on-north-koreas-kim-sources-say-idUSKCN2263DW" target="_blank">Reuters reported</a> Beijing sent a delegation led by <span style="background-color:null;">a senior member of the Chinese Communist Party&rsquo;s International Liaison Department. The team left Beijing on Thursday for an unspecified reason, but which is believed to be providing advice on Kim&#39;s health. </span></p>

<p><span style="background-color:null;">Daily NK, a South Korea-based website run by North Korean defectors, broke the news on Tuesday that Kim was recovering from a cardiovascular operation.</span></p>

<p>Seoul-based <a href="https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20200427002552325?section=nk/nk" target="_blank">Yonhap News Agency</a> reported that a special security adviser to President Moon Jae-in told Fox News on Sunday (April 26) that Kim is &quot;alive and well.&quot;</p>

      // Retry Image
      $.each($('.retry-image'), function (i, element) {
        var img = new Image;
        $(img).data('retry', 0);

        img.onload = function () {
          switch($(img).data('retry')) {
            case 0:
              $(element).attr('src', $(element).data('main_image'));
              break;
            case 1:
              $(element).attr('src', $(element).data('local_image'));
              break;

              break;

              $(element).attr('src', 'https://via.placeholder.com/500x300?text=ImageServerTemporaryFailure');
