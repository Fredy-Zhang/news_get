										<p><strong><em>Botswana National Lockdown: No Alcohol For The Next 3 Months</em></strong></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-128335" src="https://cy4ad5.c2.acecdn.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Mokgweetsi-Masisi.jpg" alt="Botswana National Lockdown" width="600" height="337" srcset="https://cy4ad5.c2.acecdn.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Mokgweetsi-Masisi.jpg 790w, https://cy4ad5.c2.acecdn.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Mokgweetsi-Masisi-300x169.jpg 300w, https://cy4ad5.c2.acecdn.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Mokgweetsi-Masisi-768x432.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></p>
<p><span style="text-transform: initial;">The Parliament of Botswana has decreed the rules and regulations pertaining to the national lockdown in a bid to contain the outbreak of the novel coronavirus.</span></p>
<p>This follows after president Mokgweetsi Masisi <span style="text-transform: initial;">proposed extending a state of emergency in the country to last six months. </span></p>
<p>The parliament resolved that the lockdown will end on the 30th of April however schools will be closed for the next 6 months.</p>
<p>The country also dealt a heavy low to the imbibers by banning the sale of alcohol  and cigarettes for 6 while months.</p>
<p>Below are the regulations set forth</p>
<p><a href="https://www.facebook.com/story.php?story_fbid=10158217839309855&amp;id=619069854" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Regulations approved by Parliament</a> means the following shall happen:</p>
<blockquote><p>1. Lockdown will end on the 30th April 2020.</p>
<p>2. Schools will be closed for 6 months.</p>
<p>3. Entry into Botswana is prohibited unless one is a citizen or resident in Botswana for 6 months, unless the President directs otherwise.</p>
<p>4. Gatherings of 2 or more people are prohibited for 6 months.</p>
<p>5. Not more than 50 people are to attend a funeral in the next 6 months.</p>
<p>7. No visits to hospitals, prisons and rehabilitation centres in the next 6 months.</p>
<p>8. Restaurants will only open for takeaway services in the next 6 months.</p>
<p>9. Alcohol and cigarettes will not be sold for 6 months.</p>
<p>10. No employee will be retrenched in the next 6 months unless the business closes.</p>
<p>11. Employees do not have the right to strike in the next 6 months.</p></blockquote>
<p>Botswana recorded 7 more coronavirus cases, including one death and there are worries that the numbers might surge if preventive action is not taken.</p>
<p>Thus far, the confirmed number of coronavirus positive cases in Botswana is 13.</p>
<p><a href="https://www.africanews.com/2020/04/09/botswana-president-in-self-isolation-after-namibia-trip/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Botswana&#8217;s MPs were all placed under compulsory quarantine</a> by the Minister of Health after it was revealed that<span style="text-transform: initial;"> a health worker who was on duty at yesterday’s Parliament session tested positive for Coronavirus. </span></p>
