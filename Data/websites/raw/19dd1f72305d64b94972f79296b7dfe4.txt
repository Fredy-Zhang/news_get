<h1 class="entry-title">Global Pandemic cause rise in gaming, as well as botting and cheating</h1>
<div class="td-module-meta-info">
<div class="td-post-author-name"><div class="td-author-by">By</div> <a href="https://micky.com.au/author/jdelossantos/">Jermaine D. Delos Santos</a><div class="td-author-line"> - </div> </div> <span class="td-post-date"><time class="entry-date updated td-module-date" datetime="2020-04-27T18:58:34+00:00">April 27, 2020</time></span>   </div>
</header>
</div>
<div class="td-post-content tagdiv-type">
<div class="td-post-featured-image"><img width="696" height="456" class="entry-thumb" src="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/hacker-2300772_1280-1-696x456.jpg?x31535" srcset="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/hacker-2300772_1280-1-696x456.jpg 696w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/hacker-2300772_1280-1-300x197.jpg 300w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/hacker-2300772_1280-1-640x420.jpg 640w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/hacker-2300772_1280-1.jpg 741w" sizes="(max-width: 696px) 100vw, 696px" alt="Botting and cheating arise coincidentally with the rise in gaming" title="Global Pandemic Cause Rise in Gaming, as Well as Botting and Cheating" /></div>
<h2>The total lockdown caused by COVID-19 has caused a rise in gaming and, along with it, players who resort to <a href="https://sea.ign.com/playerunknowns-battlegrounds-pc/159662/news/gaming-is-on-a-rise-thanks-to-the-pandemic-but-bots-and-hackers-are-sucking-the-fun-out-of-it">illicit means</a> for an unfair advantage.</h2>
<p>At nearly 20 million concurrent players, Steam has made a milestone by breaking its previous highest record.</p>
<p>Alongside the rise in the number of players who actively use Steam’s servers, however, are unscrupulous players who use bots and cheats.</p>
<p><a href="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah.jpg?x31535" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><img src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28289 lazyload" data-src="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah.jpg?x31535" alt="A YouTuber got banned after caught cheating" data-aspectratio="741/486" width="741" height="486" data-srcset="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah.jpg 741w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-300x197.jpg 300w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-696x456.jpg 696w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-640x420.jpg 640w" sizes="(max-width: 741px) 100vw, 741px" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-28289" src="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah.jpg?x31535" alt="A YouTuber got banned after caught cheating" width="741" height="486" srcset="https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah.jpg 741w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-300x197.jpg 300w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-696x456.jpg 696w, https://micky.com.au/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/Cheetah-640x420.jpg 640w" sizes="(max-width: 741px) 100vw, 741px" /></noscript></a></p>
<p>According to a survey conducted by Adjust, gamers are willing to spend $65 just to gain an upper hand in gaming with the use of a bot.</p><div class="micky-d2944b4ebff78296aa26d5c3c076ddf8" id="micky-d2944b4ebff78296aa26d5c3c076ddf8"></div>
<h3><strong>A Historical Proportion</strong></h3>
<p>Cheating in video games is not necessarily new. With cheat codes known to the public since during the early days of gaming, the trend seems to only continue to this day.</p>
<p>Players of the now classics, PlayStation and PlayStation 2, would recall their experience using outlandish cheats via GameShark codes. Bypasses that are otherwise not normally implemented into the game as <a href="https://micky.com.au/apex-legends-season-5-easter-egg-pays-tribute-to-titanfall-2/">Easter eggs</a>.</p>
<p>Although using cheats for a single-player campaign is excusable, to use the same measure on a strictly multiplayer game is downright wrong. Often causing a bad experience to others, much to a point of rage quitting due to perceived injustice.</p>
<p>The PC platform, to where Steam runs, is even especially vulnerable to hacking. Let alone Steam, which is not necessarily robust when it comes to its anti-cheating mechanism.</p>
<p>Making the issue with cheating much more concerning is how proliferated the scheme is. A little bit of Google search can easily provide access to information on how to cheat specific games on Steam.</p>
<p>While some of these illicit means are aimed at tricking those who look for them, some are actually pretty legitimate. However, those that actually work are often not free and, therefore, costs people money in exchange.</p>

<h3><strong>Corruption in Gaming</strong></h3>
<p>But, as a premise, the majority of these amazing hacks are actually potentially ‘game-breaking’ for others and give cheaters way much <a href="https://micky.com.au/50000-players-kicked-out-of-call-of-duty-warzone-for-cheating/">unfair advantage</a>.</p><div class="micky-5e940a84291170605c978b3bedb20ddf" id="micky-5e940a84291170605c978b3bedb20ddf"></div>
<p>For instance, players who use ‘wallhacks’ are able to see other players through an otherwise opaque obstruction, like walls. Another cheat would be ‘aimbots’ which automatically targets and shoots nearby enemies.</p>
<p>As any person could surmise, these cheating mechanisms are a deal-breaker for other players. Individuals who strive their hardest to win competitively in a shooting game, like Call of Duty or Counter-Strike.</p>
<p>There plenty of other hacks that are in existence, all of which never deserving a spot in the gaming community, to begin with.</p>
<p>Who would have thought that a rise in gaming would also give rise to so much cheating?</p>
